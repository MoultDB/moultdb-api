Available Methods in MoultDB API

Taxa (bulk fetch not supported)
  • /taxa?scientificName={SC_NAME} – Retrieve details of taxa of interest, where {SC_NAME} is its scientific name
  • /taxa?taxonPath={T_PATH} – Retrieve details of taxa of interest, where {T_PATH} is its MoultDB taxon path
  • /taxa?datasource={DS_NAME}&accession={ACC} – Retrieve details of a taxon of interest, where {DS_NAME} is its datasource and {ACC} is its accession
  • /taxa/{T_PATH}/direct-children – Retrieve details of direct children (first level only) of a taxon of interest, where {T_PATH} is its MoultDB taxon path
  • /taxa/{T_PATH}/lineage – Retrieve details of parent taxa of a taxon of interest, where {T_PATH} is its MoultDB taxon path
Genomes
  • /genomes – Retrieve details of all genomes
  • /genomes?taxonPath={T_PATH}&withSubspeciesGenomes={SP_G} – Retrieve details of genomes of interest, where {T_PATH} is its MoultDB taxon path and {SP_G} indicates whether genomes from its subspecies should also be included.
Genes (bulk fetch not supported)
  • /genes?geneId={GENE_ID} - Retrieve details of a gene of interest, where {GENE_ID} is its NCBI gene identifier
  • /genes?locusTag={LT_ID} – Retrieve details of a gene of interest, where {LT_ID} is its NCBI locus tag
  • /genes?proteinId={PROT_ID} – Retrieve details of a gene of interest, where {PROT_ID} is its NCBI protein ID
  • /genes?pathwayId={PATH_ID} – Retrieve details of genes involved in a moulting pathway of interest, where {PATH_ID} is its MoultDB pathway ID
  • /genes?domainId={D_ID} – Retrieve details of genes matching a domain of interest, where {D_ID} is its InterPro domain ID
  • /genes?orthogroupId={OG_ID} – Retrieve details of genes matching an orthogroup of interest, where {OG_ID} is its MoultDB orthogroup ID
  • /genes?taxonPath={T_PATH} – Retrieve details of genes of a taxon of interest, where {T_PATH} is its MoultDB taxon path
Pathways
  • /pathways – Retrieve all moulting pathways
  • /pathways?pathwayId={PATH_ID} – Retrieve details of a moulting pathway of interest, where {PATH_ID} is its MoultDB pathway ID
  • /pathways/with-orthogroups – Retrieve pathway IDs with their associated orthogroups
Orthogroups
  • /orthogroups/{OG_ID} – Retrieve details of an orthogroup of interest, where {OG_ID} is its MoultDB orthogroup ID
  • /orthogroups/moulting – Retrieve details of all orthogroups involved in a moulting pathway
Domains
  • /domains – Retrieve details of all domains
  • /domains/{D_ID} – Retrieve details of a domain of interest, where {D_ID} is its InterPro domain ID
Moulting characters annotations
  • /taxon-annotations - Retrieve all moulting characters annotations
  • /taxon-annotations/species?scientificName={SC_NAME} – Retrieve moulting characters annotations of a taxon of interest, where {SC_NAME} is its scientific name
  • /taxon-annotations/species?taxonPath={T_PATH} – Retrieve moulting characters annotations of a taxon of interest, where {T_PATH} is its MoultDB taxon path
  • /taxon-annotations/species?datasource={DS_NAME}&accession={ACC} – Retrieve moulting characters annotations of a taxon of interest, where {DS_NAME} is its datasource and {ACC} is its accession
Taxon search
  • /search/taxon_autocomplete?q={QUERY}&limit={LIMIT} - Retrieve names (scientific name or synonym) matching the search query {QUERY}, limited to {LIMIT} results (default: 100, maximum: 10000)
  • /search/taxon_search?q={QUERY}&limit={LIMIT} - Retrieve details of taxa matching the search query {QUERY}, limited to {LIMIT} results (default: 1000, maximum: 10000)
  • /search/inat_search?q={QUERY}&limit={LIMIT} - Retrieve iNaturalist accession and scientific name of taxa matching the search query {QUERY}, limited to {LIMIT} results (default: 100, maximum: 10000)
Release info
  • /release/info - Retrieve release info including some statistics

WARNING: MoultDB IDs and paths are not persistent
